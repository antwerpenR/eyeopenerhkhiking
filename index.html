App users

Like most Apps, EOHK collects non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the phone type, language preference, and the date and time of each visitor request. EOHK's purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how EOHK's visitors use its App. From time to time, EOHK may release non-personally-identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage in the App.

EOHK also collects potentially personally-identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users entering comments and managing their RSVPs. EOHK only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally-identifying information as described below, except that App commenter IP addresses and email addresses are visible and disclosed to the administrators of the App.
